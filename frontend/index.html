how it works

1

register

patients sign up through a verified hospital. researchers apply with credentials.

2

list or browse

patients list anonymized data. researchers browse available datasets.

3

consent

patients grant granular, time-limited consent. full control, always revocable.

4

earn or access

researchers pay in HEALTH tokens. patients earn 95%, platform takes 5%.
